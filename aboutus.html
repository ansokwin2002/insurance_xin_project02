Introduction

Titan Stone Life Insurance is part of Titan Stone Group, established in Phnom Penh, Cambodia in 2020. With our group’s know-how in finance and advantages, we provide professional insurance products and services based on our mission, We Care About Your Life, and the group’s principles, Honesty, Professionalism, and Safety.



We introduce the latest international asset management and insurance concepts and hope that people can understand the true meaning of buying insurance with correct financial concepts. At the same time, solid financial management courses are arranged to educate our salespersons to become your best financial planners and help you maintain a stable financial statement covering damages from risks that occur suddenly and achieving your dreams in life.



We are also committed to charity activities to support those in need. Since the outbreak of the epidemic, we have joined 9 charity activities and made donations, including 6 aid activities and 3 donations with more than 10,000 kilograms of white rice medical aids, and other donations. The supported people are across many provinces in the country.

Our goal is to help our clients maintaining financial stability and support sustainable loving assistance to create a more stable future for the people and make them realize more dreams.

Titan Stone Group

Titan Stone Group, founded in Hong Kong, in 2003, is a pioneer in exploring new markets for financial investment and has subsidiaries and offices in major cities in Asia such as Hong Kong, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Xiamen, Taipei, and Phnom Penh. Its services include investment, private banking, and insurance fields, such as overseas real estate, life insurance, equity acquisitions, asset management, and personal financial plans, etc. With a strong and professional finance team, Titan Stone Group has helped many clients customize their investment plans and increase their capital values in more than a decade.



Titan Stone Group, in recent years, participated in overseas real estate and diversified investments to provide comprehensive wealth management services with high quality for companies and clients. Because of the potential and advantages of Cambodia, Titan Stone Group started its first project in Cambodia - Picasso City Garden in 2015. Titan Stone Group has survived the difficulties caused by the epidemic, overcame the crisis of lack of labor and materials, and completed this Phnom Penh luxury residence in 2021. Currently, it also plans the development of a special economic zone and helps Cambodia's industrial upgrading and economic development with its resources to introduce a complete supply chain.



In 2020, Titan Stone Life Insurance Company was established. It hopes to assist Cambodians in financial planning and realize their dreams in life, health protection, and financial freedom through innovative financial management concepts and high-quality insurance products. At the same time, it educates local talents to become excellent financial professionals and customizes financial plans and suggestions for people to help them turn their dreams into reality.



Titan Stone Group utilizes its resources to invest in Cambodia with multiple perspectives and continues to dedicate to this country with practical actions to support local development and help everyone create a prosperous life.
